Nightwing

Batman's first sidekick. The first Robin. After his parents were murdered, Dick Grayson was taken in by Bruce Wayne as his ward and trained to become Robin. Eventually, he becomes the leader of the Teen Titans. After a disagreement with Batman, Dick trashes his Robin persona and later returns as the new hero of Bludhaven, Nightwing.

Nightwing's first appearance was in Tales of the Teen Titans Vol 1 Issue 44
